Total Solution is how we define SHD. From design (3D Digital rendering services- consultant), engineering, manufacturing and shipping, SHD provides a Total Solution to our customers helping to give them a competitive advantage in the market place.

We have mainly help polyresin statues companies to make statues like Japanese characters ( Dragon Ball, Hunter X Hunter…) , Disney figures, DC characters and other games characters.

Our team has these departments: engineering, mold opening, casting, polishing, decoration( hand drawing & painting), assembly, packaging and QC team.
